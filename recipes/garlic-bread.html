Garlic Bread With Cheese Recipe

This Garlic Bread with Cheese is made with French Bread, a delicious garlic butter mixture, and is topped with Parmesan and Mozzarella Cheese. Plus a secret ingredient that takes this recipe up to restaurant quality!

Ingredients

  • French Bread (1 loaf, about 1lb)
  • Salted butter(1 stick, room temp.)
  • Fresh garlic(6 cloves, minced)
  • italian salad dressing(1/4 cup)
  • Parmesan cheese(1/4 cup, grated)
  • shredded mozzarella cheease(3 cups, divided)
  • Fresh parsley(to garnish)

Steps

  1. Slice the bread in half to create two thinner pieces of equal size.
  2. Combine room temperature butter, minced garlic, 1 cup Mozzarella cheese, and Italian Salad Dressing. Spread it evenly over each slice of bread. Top with Parmesan, then with remaining 2 cups of Mozzarella.
  3. Bake at 350° for 10 minutes, then increase heat to 450° and baked until the top begins to brown, up to 8 more minutes.
  4. Cut into 1-inch slices, garnish with parsley, and serve with pasta, soup, or homemade marinara sauce.
