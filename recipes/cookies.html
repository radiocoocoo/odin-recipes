Simple Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

A simple chocolate chip cookies recipe

This is a simple chocolate chip cookie recipe that will turn out gooey and delicious.

Ingredients

  • Flour (200 grams)
  • Baking powder (1tbsp)
  • Butter (150 grams)
  • Sugar (40 grams)
  • Eggs (3 eggs)
  • Salt (1/2 tsp)
  • Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees and prepare your baking tray
  2. Start by combining the dry ingredients (flour, baking powder, salt) in a bowl
  3. In a separate bowl, combine sugar and butter until reaching a creamy consistency
  4. Add the eggs to the butter and sugar mixture
  5. Add the dry ingredients to your mixture and combine
  6. Add the chocolate chips and mix lightly
  7. Scoop your mixture on the baking tray leaving 2 centimeters between each scoop
  8. Place your baking tray in the over for 15 minutes or just before your cookies are fully cooked.
  9. After baking, remove from oven and leave the cookies in the baking tray for at least 10 minutes or until they cool down to prevent breakage.
